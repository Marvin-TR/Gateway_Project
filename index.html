Welcome to Mexico

Mexico's culture and multiple traditions are something that have to be lived at least once. The country is filled with multiple thing that you can visit and do. Many of these events are often related to Mexico's history and celebrating certain events.

Quick Fun Facts for Travelers!!

The country ranks fourth in the world for biodiversity

Not only is Mexico linguistically diverse, but it's also considered to be an ecologically megadiverse country

Millions of monarch butterflies migrate to Mexico every year

Every fall, millions of monarch butterflies migrate to the warm climates of Mexico to wait out the winter before returning to the U.S. and Canada in the spring.

Mexico City has over 160 museums

When you make your trip to Mexico City, you're not going to be hurting for things to do, the city has over 160 museums.
